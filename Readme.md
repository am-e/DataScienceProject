Goal:
=====
The goal of this investigation is to identify the “key statistics” of players from the provided 2013 men’s tennis tournaments that have a high correlation with the odds generated for a later tournament in 2013 and propose a method that will more accurately predict future winners of men’s tournament tennis matches.
